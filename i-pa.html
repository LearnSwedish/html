Should it be "i" or "på"?

In most cases, Swedish and English express things the same way: "Katten sitter PÅ stolen" translates directly as "The cat is sitting ON the chair.", but in some cases it turns out differently. And sometimes the meaning changes, for instance if you say "i skolan" or "på skolan".
