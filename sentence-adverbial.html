What is this?

In Swedish, a sentence adverbial — such as inte (not), alltid (always), aldrig (never) — has a specific position depending on sentence structure. In main clauses, it generally occupies the 3rd position, which means that the first two positions are taken by the subject and the verb, regardless of their order: either S1, V2, Adv3 or V1, S2, Adv3. In subclauses, the sentence adverbial takes a position before the verb, indicated by the abbreviation B4 ("before").
