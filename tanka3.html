Comparison of Swedish phrasal verbs with "tänka"


Phrasal verb Character Focus Grammar
tänka över Reflective Overall perspective, evaluation and major decisions. Transitive
tänka igenom Analytical Logical sequence, details and consequences. Transitive
tänka ut Creative Constructing a plan, idea or solution. Transitive
tänka om Revising Changing one’s view or strategy. Intransitive
tänka efter Checking A pause for careful thought or to recall information. Intransitive
tänka tillbaka Retrospective Actively recalling memories or feelings of nostalgia. Intransitive*

* But often takes a prepositional object, e.g. "tänka tillbaka på min barndom".
