A modal verb is a special kind of verb that helps other verbs. It shows if something is possible, necessary, or if someone is allowed to do something. Examples of modal verbs are can (for being able to do something), must (for something that has to be done), and may (for permission). They work together with other verbs to give extra meaning. For example, in "I can swim," "can" is the modal verb that shows you are able to swim.

Set 1: Modal verb, "vara" or "ha"? Then just repeat the verb

If a question contains a modal verb (e.g., kunna/can, ska/shall, bör/should, få/may, måste/must) or the verbs "vara" (to be) or "ha" (to have), the answer can repeat the same verb. In this regard, there is no difference between (e.g.) Swedish and English..

Swedish English
Question Short Answer Question Short Answer
Kan du simma? Ja, det kan jag. Can you swim? Yes, I can.
Ska vi gå nu? Ja, det ska vi. Shall we go now? Yes, we shall.
Borde jag träna mer? Ja, det borde du. Should I exercise more? Yes, you should.
Får jag gå ut? Ja, det får du. May I go out? Yes, you may.
Måste jag städa rummet nu? Ja, det måste du. Must I clean the room now? Yes, you must.
Är du trött? Ja, det är jag. Are you tired? Yes, I am.
Har ni sett filmen? Ja, det har vi. Have you seen the movie? Yes, we have.

Set 2: "Vilja" (to want) is a difference

However, there is a key difference between Swedish and English: the verb "vilja" (to want) functions as a modal verb in Swedish, but not in English. As a result, the short answers in the two languages differ when "vilja"/"want" is used.

Swedish English
Question Short Answer Question Short Answer
Vill du gå på bio? Ja, det vill jag. Do you want to go to the cinema? Yes, I do.

Set 3: Common Actions and Responses

For all other verbs, the short answer can be formed using the verb "att göra" (to do), and the same applies in English.

Swedish English
Question Short Answer Question Short Answer
Talar du svenska? Ja, det gör jag. Do you speak Swedish? Yes, I do.
Studerar du juridik? Ja, det gör jag. Do you study law? Yes, I do.
Lagar du mat? Ja, det gör jag. Do you cook? Yes, I do.
Sover du? Ja, det gör jag. Do you sleep? Yes, I do.
Kör du motorcykel? Ja, det gör jag. Do you ride a motorcycle? Yes, I do.
