Information

Here you practise how to say "his/her/their [own]" in Swedish. Each sentence refers to one and the same person (or group). You must choose between {hans; hennes; deras} and {sin; sitt; sina}. If the pronoun is part of the subject, choose {hans; hennes; deras}. In all other cases, use {sin; sitt; sina}.


Please note!

In this exercise, the gender of the head noun is indicated (which makes the exercise easier). If you’d like more of a challenge, try the harder version (see the menu options).
