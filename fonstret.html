About

This page shows a group of Swedish ett-gender nouns ending in -el, -en or -er.

Like other ett-gender nouns, they take the definite singular suffix -et (träd → trädet, hus → huset, barn → barnet).

However, because these words already contain an unstressed e near the end (vapen), this e is dropped before the suffix is added.

So the definite form is not vapenet, but vapnet.
