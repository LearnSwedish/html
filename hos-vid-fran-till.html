Swedish Prepositions: How They Work



Placement (where something is located)


på – being on a surface or within a defined public place.
i – being in an enclosed or clearly bounded space.
hos – being at someone’s home or premises.
vid – being next to, by, or beside something.


The prepositions “på” and “i” are slightly unusual; you can find an exercise focusing specifically on these two here .


Movement (direction or change of location)


över – moving across or over something.
mot – moving towards something.
från – moving away from a place.
till – moving to a place or destination.
genom – moving through something.
längs – moving along something.
in i – moving into an enclosed space.
in på – moving onto or into a defined area or surface.
ut ur – moving out of an enclosed space.
