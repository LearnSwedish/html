On these expressions


Vi sågs i somras. (last summer)
Specific summer — the most recent past one. Common in everyday speech. Emphasises that sth happened, usually only once.

Vi sågs under sommaren. (during the summer)
Not a specific summer. Formal style, common in writing. Emphasises that sth occurs repeatedly during a certain period.

Vi sågs på sommaren. (in summer)
Not a specific summer – rather sth one usually does in summers.

Vi sågs hela sommaren. (all the summer)
Implies "from start to finish".

Vi sågs över sommaren (?). (over the summer)
Implies that sth (without interruption) was left behind, remained untouched, or was stored unused all summer.
