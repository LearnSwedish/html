On Time Prepositions


om1

Refers to the time until sth will happen in the future. (If sth will happen in two days, it will happen "om två dagar")


om2

Used for the units "dagen" (day) and "året" (the year) to express [Number of times / Quantity] per day / per year (compare with i2).


på1

Refers to the brief time it took to complete sth. (If you only needed five minutes, you did it "på fem minuter")


på2

Refers to sth you haven't done for certain period. (If three weeks have passed, you haven’t done it "på tre veckor")


i1

Refers to the duration during which sth occurred. (If 20 minutes passed before you saw the doctor, you waited "i 20 minuter")


i2

Used with all other units ("sekunden", "minuten", "timmen", etc.) to express [Number of times / Quantity] per second / per minute / etc..


för

Refers to when sth happened in the past. (If you did sth a week ago, you did it "för en vecka sedan")
