Frequency within a time frame


This is a practice exercise focusing on how to express frequency in Swedish.
You will encounter expressions such as "tre gånger i veckan" (three times a week) or "två gånger om dagen" (twice a day), where frequency is paired with a time phrase using the appropriate preposition. The correct preposition depends on the type of time unit:


  • om for less exact periods (e.g. dagen, natten, dygnet, sommaren, vintern, året).
  • i for measurable, exact units (e.g. sekunden, minuten, timmen, veckan, månaden).
  • per and varje can be used more generally – both work with almost all time units.
