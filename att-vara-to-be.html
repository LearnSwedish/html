Information

"Att vara" (to be) is an irregular verb in the Swedish language. It does not follow the standard conjugation patterns of regular verbs and has several unique forms across different tenses. This exercise focuses on learning how the verb is used in various sentences. You can hover over all the words for a translation into English. When you hover over a dropdown menu, you see the English equivalent of the verb's tense and thus which tense you should select for that specific sentence. Good luck!
