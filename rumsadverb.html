Spatial adverbials


Swedish uses three-way systems to express (1) a state or position (i.e. här, here, (2) movement towards something (i.e. hit, to this place (here), and (3) movement away from something (i.e. härifrån, from this place (from here).


1. Location (static position)

  • här = here
  • där = there
  • hemma = at home
  • borta = gone

2. Movement to a position

  • hit = to here (cf. old-fashioned English hither)
  • dit = to there (cf. old-fashioned English thither)
  • hem = (to) home
  • bort = away

3. Movement from that position

  • härifrån = from here
  • därifrån = from there
  • hemifrån = from home
  • bortifrån = from far away

Tip: The shortest form (with the exception of "vart", where to) always expresses movement towards something. And anything that expresses movement from something will always contain the element "från".
