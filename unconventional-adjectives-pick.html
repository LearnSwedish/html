Unconventional adjectives: pick the correct form

Read the "Info" to understand what this exercise is about.

Information

In this exercise, you will familiarise yourself with 27
unconventional Swedish adjectives like ny (new), röd (red),
glad (happy) and hård (hard) – unconventional in the sense
that they do not follow the standard pattern of inflection,
which is for härlig (lovely), grön (green), ljus (light) etc:


  • N gender: härlig, which equals the base form
  • T gender: base form +t » härligt
  • Plural: base form +a » härliga

 

Instead, we see patterns such as (new) "ny, NYTT, nya" and
(beautiful) "vacker, vackert VACKRA". You'll get used to it! :)
Good luck!
