Information

This exercise focuses on learning to correctly identify and use Swedish participles. Participants must select the appropriate participle form – either present or perfect – based on the context of each sentence. The activity includes indefinite and definite forms, offering practice with grammar rules such as gender and number agreement, vowel changes in strong verbs and recognising the nuances between present participles (-ande or -ende) and perfect participles in various contexts.
