Information

This exercise focuses on learning to correctly identify and use Swedish participles. For each sentence, select the appropriate participle form (three of the options will be wrong) based on the context of each sentence. The activity includes indefinite and definite forms, offering practice with grammar rules such as gender and number agreement, vowel changes in strong verbs and recognising the nuances between present participles (-ande or -ende) and perfect participles in various contexts.


If you need to practise how Swedish participles work,
click on the "The logic" button.


If you don't understand any of the Swedish sentences, just hover over the dropdown and a translation will be shown, in which the English equivalent of the participle is underlined.
