About this exercise

This flip-card exercise features 725 carefully selected words — the most essential ones according to research for language learners. For nouns, you'll learn the gender and plural forms. For verbs, you'll see their conjugations across different tenses. For adjectives, you'll find their forms based on gender and pluralization.

 

The card can be flipped either by clicking on it or by clicking the "Flip" button. To proceed to the next word, click the "Next" button

 

The words are presented in random order, and during the same session, no word appears twice.
