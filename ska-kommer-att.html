Ska eller kommer att?


Rule 1: If the sentence expresses a prediction, guess, or forecast, choose kommer att.


Rule 2: If the sentence expresses an intention, plan, decision, or obligation, choose ska.


Exceptions to Rule 2:

  • To sound softer or more polite, especially when it affects another person, use kommer att.
  • If the subject is inanimate or an animal, use kommer att.


Note

In everyday speech, [ska + infinitive] and [kommer att + infinitive] often overlap in use.


Vi ska gifta oss i sommar.
Vi kommer att gifta oss i sommar.


Both forms are correct, but the first sounds more factual, while the second sounds more personal and emotionally expressive.
