Unconventional adjectives: pick the correct form

Read the "Info" to understand what this exercise is about.

Information

In this exercise, you will familiarise yourself with 23 unconventional Swedish adjectives – unconventional in the sense that they do not follow the standard pattern of inflection, which is (for "härlig", lovely):


  • N gender: härlig, which equals the base form
  • T gender: base form +t » härligt
  • Plural: base form +a » härliga

 

Instead, we see patterns such as (new) "ny, NYTT, nya" and (beautiful) "vacker, vackert, VACKRA". You'll get used to it! :) Good luck!
