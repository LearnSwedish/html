Information

In this exercise, you will familiarise yourself with 32 unconventional Swedish adjectives – unconventional in the sense that they do not follow the standard pattern of inflection, which is:

  • N gender: härlig, which equals the base form)
  • T gender: base form +t, härligt
  • Plural: base form +a, härliga

Instead, we see patterns such as "ny, NYTT, nya" and "vacker, vackert, VACKRA". You'll get used to it! :) Good luck!
