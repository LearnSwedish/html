Information

About 15% of Swedish adjectives are non-regular (i.e. irregular, inflexible or defective), meaning they do not follow the straightforward inflection pattern, which is basic (en gender) form, neuter +t, and plural +a. However, once you have mastered these 15%, you have effectively mastered all adjectives! Select the correct form to ensure it agrees with the noun it describes.
