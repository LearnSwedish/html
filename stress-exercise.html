Some basics about Swedish word stress

1. Suffixes that attract stress

Primary stress (Main stress):

  • The suffix "-ik" almost always carries the primary stress.
    Examples: politik, teknik, musik, kritik, praktik, metodik.
  • The suffix "-i" (derived from Latin and Greek) almost always carries the primary stress.
    Examples: energi, ekonomi, akademi, magi, sympati, demokrati, strategi, filosofi.
  • The suffixes "-tion" and "-sion" (from Latin) carry the primary stress.
    Examples: funktion, situation, tradition, vegetation.
  • The suffix "-ell" carries the primary stress.
    Examples: professionell, funktionell, traditionell, speciell.

Secondary stress:

Final suffixes such as "-bar", "-het", "-sam", "-skap", "-full", "-lös", and "-vis" almost always carry secondary stress. Importantly, these suffixes tend to "override" previous secondary stresses in the word (see section 3).

2. The "Step-back Rule"

When a word takes an ending such as "-er" or "-isk" — for example, politik becoming politiker, or ekonomi becoming ekonomisk — the stress often shifts one syllable to the left.

po-lit-IK » po-LI-tik-er
e-ko-nom-I » e-ko-NOM-isk

3. Unstable Secondary Stress

Many Swedish words are polysyllabic and feature a primary stress (marked 1... below) followed by one or more secondary stresses (marked 2... below):

ar-be-te » 1ar-2be-te
an-stän-dig » 1an-2stän-dig
fort-sätt-ning » 1fort-2sätt-ning

The Displacement Rule:

If you add a "heavy" suffix (like -lös, -het, -skap) to these words, the new suffix takes over the secondary stress, causing the previous one to disappear.

Example: 1an-2stän-dig » 1an-stän-dig-2het.

A common mistake for learners is to keep the original secondary stress while also stressing the new suffix. For instance, while it is correct ✅ to say 1fort-2sätt-ning, it is incorrect ❌ to say 1fort-2sätt-nings-2vis. The correct pronunciation is 1fort-sätt-nings-2vis.

Note that the secondary stress moves further down the line each time a new heavy suffix is added:

mått-lig » 1mått-2lig
mått-lig-het » 1mått-lig-2het
mått-lig-hets-full » 1mått-lig-hets-2full
