What's the definite form?

Read the "Info" to understand what this exercise is about.

(en) stol

Apply these four rules

The majority of en-words take the ending -en in the definite form

gren [branch] » gren-en [the branch]

and most ett-words take the ending -et in the definite form

hus [house] » hus-et [the house]

However, this does not apply to en-words ending in -el, -er, and -or, nor to ett-words ending in -el and -er. In this exercise, you will get a delightful mix of both common and slightly special nouns. Choose the correct definite form. If you don't understand a word, just hover over it.
